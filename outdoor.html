STUNNING ENTERTAINING SPACES

Patios & Entertaining Areas

Transform your outdoor concrete into a beautiful, durable entertaining space with our UV-resistant epoxy coatings. Our outdoor epoxy systems are specifically formulated to withstand the intense Pacific Northwest weather, including moisture, temperature fluctuations, and UV exposure. The result is a stunning patio surface that looks fresh and beautiful for years to come.

Whether you're hosting family gatherings or outdoor events, a properly coated patio provides the perfect backdrop while maintaining its vibrant finish through countless seasons and weather cycles.

  • UV-resistant to prevent fading and yellowing
  • Moisture-resistant formulation for wet climates
  • Customizable colors and decorative options
  • Easy to clean and maintain year-round

SAFE & DECORATIVE

Walkways & Driveways

Outdoor walkways and driveways need more than just durability—they need safety. Our slip-resistant epoxy coatings provide superior traction even in rainy conditions, protecting family and guests from slips and falls. The textured, slip-resistant surface grips your feet while remaining visually stunning.

Driveways especially benefit from our epoxy protection, which shields concrete from oil stains, chemical damage, and weathering while creating an attractive entryway to your home. With decorative flake options and custom colors, your driveway becomes a design statement.

  • Slip-resistant texture for safety in rain and moisture
  • Prevents oil and stain absorption
  • Decorative flake and color options available
  • Extends concrete lifespan significantly

LUXURY POOL SPACES

Pool Decks & Outdoor Recreation

Pool decks demand special consideration due to constant water exposure, UV intensity, and foot traffic. Our cool-to-the-touch epoxy coatings are engineered to remain comfortable even in direct sun while providing excellent slip resistance on wet surfaces. This creates the perfect balance of safety, comfort, and luxury aesthetics.

Beyond just functionality, our decorative flake options let you create stunning, resort-style pool deck designs. Choose from elegant color combinations that complement your outdoor living space and reflect light beautifully in the sun.

  • Cool-to-the-touch formula reduces surface temperature
  • Superior slip resistance when wet
  • Beautiful decorative flake design options
  • Chlorine and chemical resistant formulation
