MOST POPULAR

Garage Floors

Your garage is more than just a place to park your car. It's a workshop, a storage area, and often an extension of your home. Epoxy garage flooring is the most popular residential application for good reason—it transforms a dull concrete slab into a showroom-quality space that's incredibly durable and easy to maintain.

Whether you choose a bold flake design, sleek solid color, or eye-catching metallic finish, your new epoxy floor will make your garage a space you're proud to show off. Epoxy is resistant to stains, spills, and chemicals, so oil spots, rust, and salt won't damage your floor.

  • Slip-resistant surface for safety
  • Hides dirt and minor concrete imperfections
  • Resistant to oil, grease, and automotive stains
  • Wide range of colors and design options
  • Extremely durable for heavy use
  • Simple to clean and maintain

Basement & Living Spaces

Basements often feel cold, dark, and uninviting—but with epoxy flooring, you can completely transform the space into a beautiful, functional area for your family. Epoxy creates a finished, polished look that makes basements feel more like intentional living spaces rather than storage areas.

Our epoxy systems are moisture-resistant, which means they'll hold up in basements where humidity and occasional water exposure can be concerns. Combine epoxy with proper moisture management, and you'll have a basement that stays dry, looks beautiful, and adds real value to your home. Perfect for recreational rooms, home offices, gyms, or guest quarters.

  • Moisture-resistant finish
  • Creates a polished, finished appearance
  • Warmer, more inviting feel than bare concrete
  • Easy to keep clean and bright
  • Adds genuine value to your home
  • Perfect for recreation or living areas

SEAMLESS PROTECTION

Laundry Rooms & Utility Areas

Laundry rooms, utility areas, and other functional spaces in your home face unique challenges—water splashes, detergent spills, and high foot traffic. Epoxy flooring is the perfect solution because it's completely seamless, making it impossible for liquids to seep through cracks or damage the concrete underneath.

Unlike tile with grout lines that stain and accumulate dirt, epoxy is one continuous surface that's incredibly easy to clean. A quick mop is all you need to keep it spotless. Epoxy is also resistant to chemicals, stains, and moisture, so your utility spaces stay protected and looking fresh for decades.

  • Seamless, impermeable surface
  • Protects against spills and stains
  • Resistant to detergents and chemicals
  • No grout lines to trap dirt
  • Fast and easy to clean
  • Long-lasting durability and protection
