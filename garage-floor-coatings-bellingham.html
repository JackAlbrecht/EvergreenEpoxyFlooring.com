Garage Floor Coating Systems

Flake Epoxy Garage Floor Coatings

Our most popular garage floor coating system. Flake epoxy (also called chip epoxy or decorative flake) combines a high-build epoxy base coat with broadcast decorative vinyl flakes and a clear polyurea or polyaspartic topcoat. The result is a garage floor coating that is incredibly durable, slip-resistant, easy to clean, and available in hundreds of color combinations. Flake garage floor coatings hide imperfections in the concrete and provide a professional look that transforms any garage. Most flake garage floor coatings are completed in just 2 days.

Metallic Epoxy Garage Floor Coatings

For homeowners wanting a premium, one-of-a-kind garage floor, our metallic epoxy garage floor coating creates stunning three-dimensional flowing patterns with a rich shimmer effect. Each metallic epoxy garage floor is completely unique. This premium garage floor coating system uses metallic pigments suspended in high-performance epoxy that shift and flow during application, creating a look similar to polished marble or flowing lava. Metallic epoxy garage floors take 3-4 days to install due to the specialized application technique.

Solid Color Garage Floor Coatings

A clean, uniform garage floor coating in a single color. Solid color epoxy garage floor coatings are ideal for homeowners who want a professional, workshop-style look. Available in dozens of colors from classic gray to bold tones, solid color garage floor coatings provide excellent protection and easy maintenance at an affordable price point.

Quartz Garage Floor Coatings

Broadcast quartz garage floor coatings provide maximum slip resistance and durability. The quartz aggregate creates a textured surface that maintains traction even when wet, making it ideal for garages in Bellingham's rainy climate. Quartz garage floor coatings are also extremely chemical-resistant, perfect for workshops and automotive garages.

Our Garage Floor Coating Process

Every Evergreen garage floor coating project follows our proven 3-step process:

Day 1 - Surface Preparation: We diamond-grind your entire garage floor to create the ideal surface profile for coating adhesion. We repair all cracks, fill spalls, and perform moisture testing. Proper surface preparation is the #1 factor in garage floor coating longevity, and we never skip or shortcut this critical step.

Day 2 - Coating Application: We apply the base coat, decorative flakes or metallic pigments, and begin the topcoat application. Our Leggari brand coating systems provide superior adhesion, chemical resistance, and UV stability compared to big-box store garage floor coating kits.

Day 3 - Topcoat & Curing: Final clear topcoat application and curing. Your garage floor coating is ready for light foot traffic in 24 hours and full vehicle use within 72 hours.

Garage Floor Coating vs. DIY Garage Floor Paint

Many homeowners compare professional garage floor coatings to DIY garage floor paint kits from hardware stores. Here is why professional garage floor coatings from Evergreen outperform DIY every time:

Professional garage floor coatings use commercial-grade 100% solids epoxy with polyurea or polyaspartic topcoats. They include diamond grinding surface preparation, multi-layer application, and come with a 15-year warranty. Professional coatings are 10-20x thicker than paint, resist hot tire pickup, and last 15-20+ years.

DIY garage floor paint kits use water-based epoxy paint that is 1-3 mils thick (vs. 20-30+ mils for professional coatings). They require only acid etching (inferior adhesion), peel within 1-3 years, yellow from UV exposure, and come with no meaningful warranty. The average DIY garage floor coating fails within 2 years.

Garage Floor Coating FAQ

How long does a garage floor coating last?

Professional garage floor coatings from Evergreen last 15-20+ years. We use Leggari brand commercial-grade coating systems and back every garage floor with our 15-year warranty. DIY kits typically fail within 1-3 years.

Can you coat a garage floor in winter?

Yes. We install garage floor coatings year-round in Bellingham and Whatcom County. Our coating systems cure at temperatures as low as 50 degrees F, and we use portable heating when needed to ensure proper curing during colder months.

How long before I can park on my new garage floor coating?

Light foot traffic is safe after 24 hours. You can move furniture and light items after 48 hours. Full vehicle traffic and heavy use is safe after 72 hours (3 days) from final topcoat application.

Will epoxy garage floor coating peel?

Not when installed properly. Professional garage floor coatings from Evergreen do not peel because we diamond-grind every surface for maximum adhesion, perform moisture testing, and use commercial-grade 100% solids epoxy. Peeling is caused by inadequate surface preparation (acid etching only) and low-quality coating products, which is common with DIY kits and budget contractors.

We offer hundreds of color options. Our color chart page shows our full selection of flake blends, metallic pigments, and solid colors. Custom color matching is available for commercial projects.
