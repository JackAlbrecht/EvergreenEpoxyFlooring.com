Frequently Asked Questions

How much does epoxy flooring cost in Custer? +

Epoxy flooring costs typically range from $8 to $20 per square foot in Custer, depending on the type of epoxy system, design complexity, and concrete condition. Flake floors typically run $8-$12, solid color systems $8-$15, quartz systems $12-$15, and metallic floors $15-$20. We provide free estimates tailored to your project.

How long does epoxy installation take? +

Most residential epoxy flooring projects in Custer take 3-7 days from start to finish, including surface preparation, application, and curing. Commercial projects may take longer depending on square footage. We'll provide a specific timeline during your free estimate.

What areas of Whatcom County do you serve? +

We serve Custer, Burlington, Sedro-Woolley, La Conner, Bow, and throughout Whatcom County. We also service Whatcom County and Island County. Contact us for a free estimate in your area.

Is epoxy flooring durable? +

Yes, epoxy flooring is extremely durable and can last 20+ years with proper maintenance. It resists chemicals, stains, moisture, and heavy traffic. In Custer garages and commercial spaces, epoxy stands up to intense use and environmental conditions.

Can I install epoxy in my Custer basement? +

Absolutely. Epoxy is ideal for Custer basements because it's moisture-resistant and creates a finished, polished look. We use moisture management techniques to ensure long-lasting performance in damp environments.

How do I maintain my epoxy floor? +

Epoxy floors are very easy to maintain. Simply sweep and mop regularly with mild soap and water. Avoid dragging heavy objects and use floor protectors under furniture. We provide detailed care instructions after installation to maximize the life of your floor.
