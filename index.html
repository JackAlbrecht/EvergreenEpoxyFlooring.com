COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does epoxy flooring cost in Bellingham, WA? +

Epoxy flooring in the Bellingham and Whatcom County area typically ranges from $8-$20 per square foot depending on the system and condition of the floor. Flake floors generally run $8-$12, solid color floors $8-$15, quartz systems $12-$15, and metallic floors $15-$20. Every project is different, so contact us for a free personalized quote.

How long does epoxy flooring installation take? +

Flake epoxy floors typically take 2 days to install. Metallic epoxy floors require 3-4 days due to the additional coats and specialized technique involved. Full curing takes about a week after installation before heavy use.

What areas does Evergreen Epoxy Flooring serve? +

We serve all of Whatcom County (Bellingham, Ferndale, Lynden, Blaine, Everson), Skagit County (Mount Vernon, Anacortes, Burlington, Sedro-Woolley, La Conner), and Island County (Oak Harbor, Coupeville, Langley, Camano Island) in Washington State.

Do you offer epoxy flooring training? +

Yes! We offer professional epoxy flooring training through Evergreen Epoxy Training. Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or an aspiring epoxy professional, our hands-on training programs will teach you everything you need to know. Visit evergreenepoxytraining.com to learn more.

What makes Leggari epoxy products different? +

Leggari products are 100% solids, made in the USA, and professional-grade. Unlike big box store kits that are 40-60% solids (meaning the rest evaporates), Leggari's full-solid formulas create thicker, more durable coatings that last significantly longer. Read more about why we choose Leggari.

Which epoxy system is best for my garage? +

For most residential garages, flake epoxy is the best value—it's affordable, durable, hides dirt, provides slip resistance, and installs in just 2 days. Metallic epoxy is ideal if you want a luxury statement floor. Read our full comparison guide.
