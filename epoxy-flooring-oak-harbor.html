Frequently Asked Questions - Oak Harbor

How long does epoxy flooring installation take? +
Most residential garage floors take 1-2 days. Commercial projects may take 2-5 days depending on size and complexity.
How much does epoxy flooring cost in Oak Harbor? +
Pricing depends on size, system chosen, and concrete condition. We offer free estimates - call (360) 220-7631 for a personalized quote.
Is epoxy good for Island County weather? +
Yes! Our Leggari epoxy systems handle Pacific Northwest moisture, temperature swings, and heavy use. Chemical-resistant, UV-stable, and built to last decades.
Do you offer warranties? +
Yes! We stand behind every installation with comprehensive warranty options. Ask about details during your free consultation.
Can epoxy be applied over old concrete? +
In most cases, yes. We assess concrete condition during our free estimate visit. Minor cracks can be repaired as part of preparation.
