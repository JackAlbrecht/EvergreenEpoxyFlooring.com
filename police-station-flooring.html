Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of a police station can be coated?

We can scope flooring for sally ports, booking areas, evidence intake, locker rooms, vehicle bays, hallways, and public-facing spaces with the right texture and cleanability for each area.

Is epoxy a good fit for 24-7 law enforcement facilities?

Yes. Seamless epoxy flooring is a strong fit for police facilities because it handles constant foot traffic, carts, rolling loads, and repeated cleaning better than many traditional finishes.

Can installation be phased around operations?

Yes. We can break the work into phases so critical spaces stay accessible and the project fits real operational constraints.
