Why We Only Use Leggari Products

February 28, 20266 min read
← Back to Blog

We've been installing epoxy floors for years, and we've seen every product on the market. We've tried competing brands, we've looked at cheaper alternatives, and we've watched DIY kits promise professional results. And we keep coming back to Leggari. There's a reason we've committed to Leggari products exclusively—it's not marketing, it's experience.

The Problem with Big Box Store Epoxy

Walk into any home improvement store and you'll find shelves full of epoxy flooring kits. They're cheap, they're accessible, and they promise professional results in your garage over a weekend. Here's the reality: most of those products are water-based epoxy or epoxy-like coatings that cut corners to hit a price point. They're designed to be just good enough that homeowners will think they got a deal.

The problem is durability. Water-based epoxy coatings lack the hardness and chemical resistance of true 100% solids epoxy. They don't cure as hard. They don't stand up to UV exposure. And they're much more likely to fail, peel, or chalk within 3-5 years. That's why we don't use them.

What Makes Leggari Different

100% Solids Formulation

Leggari epoxy is 100% solids, meaning there are no water or solvent fillers. Everything that goes down becomes part of the final coating. This results in superior hardness, durability, and chemical resistance. Water-based alternatives lose volume as they dry, resulting in thinner, weaker coatings. Leggari products deliver thickness and performance that actually matters.

Made in the USA

Leggari manufactures their products right here in America with rigorous quality control. You're not buying imported products with inconsistent formulations or questionable sourcing. American manufacturing means American standards, American oversight, and accountability if something goes wrong.

Professional-Grade Engineering

Leggari products are engineered for professional installers who care about their reputation. Every product is formulated for the right viscosity, pot life, cure schedule, and hardness. The products work with our equipment and application methods because they were designed for professionals, not as a one-size-fits-all consumer product.

Training and Support

Leggari doesn't just sell products—they support their installers with training, technical support, and ongoing education. When we have questions about a specific application or problem, we can reach out to their team. That level of support matters when you're trying to deliver the best possible results for your customers.

The Difference in Real-World Performance

Durability

Leggari epoxy coatings routinely last 15-20+ years in commercial and residential applications. We're not talking about floors that look okay for a couple years and then start showing wear. We're talking about floors that stay hard, glossy, and resistant to damage for decades. That longevity translates directly to value for you—you're paying once for a floor that lasts, not replacing it every few years.

Chemical Resistance

True 100% solids epoxy from Leggari handles oils, gasoline, brake fluid, cleaning chemicals, and harsh solvents without breaking down. Try spilling diesel fuel on a big box store water-based epoxy and see what happens. The coating starts to soften and fail. Leggari stands up to real industrial and commercial abuse.

Hardness and Topical Wear

The hardness difference between 100% solids epoxy and water-based alternatives is tangible. Leggari coatings are harder, meaning they resist scratching, scuffing, and topical wear better. Your floor maintains its finish longer. This matters in high-traffic areas where appearance degrades quickly with inferior products.

Our Commitment to Leggari

We've chosen to use exclusively Leggari Products because it aligns with our commitment to delivering results that last. If we used cheap epoxy from a big box store, we could quote lower prices and complete jobs faster. But then you'd be calling us back in 3-4 years when your floor starts failing. That's not how we do business.

By using Leggari exclusively, we know exactly what we're working with. We know how the product behaves in different temperatures and humidity levels. We know its pot life and cure schedule. We know how it will perform over 20 years. We've built our reputation on it, and we stand behind every floor we install with Leggari products.

The Investment Makes Sense

Yes, professional epoxy using quality products costs more than a DIY kit from a big box store. But compare the costs over a 20-year timeline. One professional installation with Leggari products versus replacing a failed DIY job, or patching up a cheap epoxy floor every few years. The math is simple: professional-grade products and installation cost less over time.

When you invest in a Leggari floor from Evergreen Epoxy Flooring, you're paying for durability, chemical resistance, hardness, and a proven track record. You're paying for a product that's been engineered for professional use and backed by a company that stands behind what they make. That's not an expense—that's value.
