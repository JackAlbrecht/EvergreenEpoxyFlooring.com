Frequently Asked Questions - Oak Harbor

What is a concrete overlay? +
A concrete overlay is a thin layer of polymer-modified concrete applied over existing concrete. It restores the surface, hides imperfections, and can be customized with colors and textures.
How thick is a concrete overlay? +
Most overlays range from 1/4 inch to 1/2 inch thick. This is enough to cover surface damage, stains, and minor cracks while maintaining door clearances.
Can overlays fix cracked concrete? +
Overlays can cover minor surface cracks. Structural cracks need to be addressed before overlay application. We assess this during our free estimate.
How much does a concrete overlay cost in Oak Harbor? +
Overlay costs depend on area size, surface condition, and design choices. Call (360) 220-7631 for a free estimate.
How long does a concrete overlay last? +
Professional concrete overlays typically last 10-15+ years with proper maintenance, depending on traffic and exposure.
