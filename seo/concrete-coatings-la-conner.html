Frequently Asked Questions - La Conner

What types of concrete coatings do you offer in La Conner? +
We offer flake epoxy, metallic epoxy, quartz broadcast, solid color epoxy, polyaspartic coatings, and concrete overlay systems. Each system is suited for different applications and budgets.
How long do concrete coatings last? +
Our professional-grade concrete coatings typically last 15-20+ years with proper maintenance. We use premium Leggari products that are built to withstand heavy traffic and harsh conditions.
Are concrete coatings slippery? +
Our flake and quartz systems include built-in slip resistance. We can also add anti-slip additives to any coating system for areas that get wet or need extra traction.
How much do concrete coatings cost in La Conner? +
Costs vary based on the system, square footage, and concrete condition. Contact us at (360) 220-7631 for a free estimate tailored to your La Conner project.
Do I need to move everything out of my garage? +
Yes, we'll need the space cleared before we begin. We can discuss logistics during your free estimate to make the process as smooth as possible.
